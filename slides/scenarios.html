Scenarios

Precautions will vary by assumed threat-level. It's always better to be over-cautious.

Border crossing

Borders are strange, sometimes considered 'constitution-free' zones (DHS's 200 mile buffer).

  • Use strong passwords on your phone lockscreen.
  • Newer versions of the OS promise full encrytion, be sure to upgrade.

    • Carry a cheap blank burner phone and pay cash for a SIM in the other country.

National rules worth noting

CBSA claims the power to compel individuals for their passwords.

Meeting sources

Don't carry anything that transmits. Leave your phone behind, don't use your personal vehicle.

Transporting files

  • Encrypt the drive (USB Key or external harddrive.)
  • Encrypt the files
